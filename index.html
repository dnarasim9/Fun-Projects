Why am I awesome

Below are 3 of my Unique Selling Propositions

A Pack Animal

I am your friendly companion in mountainous areas. I carry about 100 pounds for you. With my two toed foot, I am agile and sure-footed. The soft pad on the bottom of my foot results in a low impact on the environment. I am easily trained and rather inexpensive to keep

A Guard Animal

I can be used as your guard animal for protecting herds of sheep, goats or geese. Using me as a guard has eliminated the losses to predators for many producers. The value of the livestock saved each year more than exceeds my purchase cost and annual maintenance cost

Exquisite Fiber

Fiber is my prized product. My lanolin-free wool is fine, soft, and warm - greatly appreciated by spinners, knitters, and weavers. Many commercial processors specialize in cleaning, carding, spinning and can turn this luxurious raw fiber into rovings and yarns

Now, a few Fun Facts!



1. I am a South American relative of the camel, though I do not have a hump

2. My fibres are fireproof

3. A male llama is called as sire, the female is named as dam and cria is the name given to baby llama

4. I come in various colours - white, brown, grey, black and beige

5. Just like camels, I can survive for weeks without water because I collect moisture from the food that I eat

6. Most of the animals bite when agitated, but I spit. Yes, if anyone disturbs me, then I spit on them

7. Finally, my manure is almost odourless
